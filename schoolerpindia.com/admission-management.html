Admission Management

Our school management software makes handling admissions easy. It helps schools collect and organize student applications, so you don't have to deal with piles of paperwork. You can quickly access and review candidate information in one place. We also send automatic updates to applicants about their admission status, making the process smooth and clear. This system saves time and makes enrolling students a breeze for schools.

Benefits of Online Admission / Enrollment

  • Fill Application form from Anywhere and Anytime.
  • No need to stand in long queues
  • Cost savings for the institutes.
  • Reducing the unwanted data.
  • Eliminates the need for extra staff to manage applicants.
  • No longer requirement of printing & storing the forms separately.

Student Enrollment

School ERP Education Management offers a cloud-based Online Enrollment and Admission Process, simplifying student registration for parents. Admin oversees and expedites this process, providing online registration forms for schools to achieve their enrollment goals. Parents submit the Online Admission Form with their child's personal and academic details, along with relevant certificates and photos.

The administrator verifies the uploaded documents and proceeds with the application. Fee payment is made hassle-free through the School ERP Education Management Online Payment Gateway; no in-person visits are required. Our mobile app streamlines access to the admission process on iOS and Android devices, enhancing communication via email and SMS notifications.

This system aims to reduce administrative costs by 30%, cutting down on manual paperwork and workloads for staff. It offers efficient enrollment, saving time and money while improving overall communication and effectiveness.
